Do I need a logo or brand first?+

No. If there is no usable identity, the page can use a restrained type-led direction. Creating a logo or complete brand system is outside this sprint.

Are the domain and hosting included?+

No. Domain registration, hosting and paid third-party costs are separate. Client-owned services can be connected when the required access is supplied.

What counts as a genuine business?+

The client must be able to supply a real business identity, accurate service and area details, a working contact route, and permission to use all supplied content.

Will the page guarantee leads or rankings?+

No. The sprint creates a clear, technically sound first page. Search visibility, traffic, enquiries and sales depend on wider factors and are not guaranteed.
