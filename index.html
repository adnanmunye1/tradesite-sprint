What does “48 hours” cover?+

The first staging build is delivered within 48 hours of receiving the booking payment, agreed content, assets and required access. Revision timing depends on when feedback arrives.

Will this guarantee more leads?+

No. The sprint improves clarity, hierarchy, speed and enquiry routes, but traffic, rankings and conversions depend on factors outside one page and are not guaranteed.

What if the site needs more than one page?+

The audit will flag it. This fixed sprint stays focused on one page; any additional work would need a separate written scope and price.
